Community

RichWHAT

Vision: RICHWHAT envisions a new Richmond reputation of peace, talent, unity and rich culture that overcomes stereotypes as well as statistics.

Mission: RICHWHAT will change the perception of Richmond and its people through a showcase of talents. The reunion of people will not only be an opportunity for people to demonstrate their abilities but to show that Richmond isn't just about violence and drug endorsement. Together we shall portray Richmond's reality, a diverse and intelligent youth that will commit to change a better well being for the generations to come.

Outcome: This event occured on May 22nd, 2015 in Richmond. There were a total of 50 people involved in the event. As members of the Youth Leadership Committee of Richmond, Luis Martinez and I codirected this event with a grant from the city of Richmond and with the help of our family and friends.

Citywide Pride


Find us on Facebook

Vision: A city with clean streets and devotedly proud neighbors.

Mission: Bringing together a diverse group of neighbors to organize a trash pickup and community celebration in Richmond.

Outcome: Citywide Pride's beautification event took place on November 1st, 2014. Approximately 50 Richmond residents volunteered early in the morning to clean the streets. Collectively, we picked up about 840 pounds of trash! We received food and donations from the city of Richmond and from residents. Special thank you to the leadership team who made this possible: Bianca Delgado, Meghan Lorenzo, and Ariana Ortega.

School

CODE 101

This club is dedicated to sparking MWA students' interest in STEM. Currently, it has 15 members who are exploring different STEM fields to find which one interests them the most. I started this club with a group of friends in order to help other students see the possibilities of a background in technology. We meet once a week during lunch to talk about software/hardware and tech careers.

KIWINS

At MWA, Kiwins is a club dedicated to connecting MWA students to community service projects. We are a part of the Jade Division. As a senior, I will be serving as Vice President for the club.

FINANCE AND INVESTMENT

This club is dedicated to teaching students financial literacy through videos and virtual experience with the stock market. During my senior year, I will be serving as the club's Secretary.

Associated Student Body Government (ASB)

MWA student body has elected me as their Vice President for the 2015-16 school year.
